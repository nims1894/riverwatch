## Mission

---

## Doctrine

> Observe the River.  
> Adapt the Boat.  
> Continue the Voyage.

---

## Purpose

RiverWatch is a personal doctrine execution system designed to help a RiverWatcher reach the Open Sea on time and on plan.

It does not try to predict the river.  
It watches the river, evaluates the boat, and checks whether the voyage remains on course.

---

## Core Health Architecture

### 🧭 Voyage Health

The top-level KPI.

> Am I still on course to reach the Open Sea?

Key outputs:

* Current Position
* Expected Position
* Open Sea Target
* Projected Asset
* ETA
* Remaining Time

---

### 🌊 River Health

Environmental assessment.

> Is the river still healthy?

Metrics:

* Fed Rate
* VIX
* Brent Oil
* USD/KRW
* AI CAPEX
* NVDA Data Center Revenue
* M2

---

### ⛵ Boat Health

Strategy suitability assessment.

> Is my boat suitable for the current river?

Structure:

* Configuration Alignment: 30%
* Suitability: 70%
  * Growth Suitability: 50%
  * Defensive Suitability: 40%
  * Maneuverability: 10%
