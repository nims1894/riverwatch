# RiverWatch Design Doctrine v1.1

1. Information must serve a decision.
2. Redundant information shall be removed.
3. Dashboard answers now.
4. Boatyard answers balance.
5. Logbook answers meaning.
6. Comfort over density.
7. Consistency over creativity.
8. Automate what changes often.
9. Manualize what changes rarely.
10. Stay the Course.
11. Calm over excitement.

## Animation Policy

- Hover color change: allowed
- Tab selection highlight: allowed
- Card lift: excluded
- Fade-in: excluded
- Slide transition: excluded
- Graph animation: excluded
- Parallax: excluded
- Count-up number: excluded
